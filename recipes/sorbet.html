Sorbet

This limoncello sorbet recipe is a concoction I came up with when I couldn't find quite what I was looking for. It is absolutely foolproof. It's simple, sweet, and tart, with just enough limoncello liqueur. Serve with a sprig of mint or fresh berries. Top with limoncello.

Ingrediants:

  • 2 cups cold water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1 lemon, zested, or more to taste
  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup limoncello

Steps

  1. Mix everything
  2. Put it in the freezer for 3 hours
  3. Eat it.
