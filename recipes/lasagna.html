Ingrediants:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (8 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (14 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Make the beshamelle
  3. Put some noodles in a plate
  4. Put some sauce over it
  5. Put some beshamelle over it
  6. Repeat steps 3-5
  7. Send it to the oven for some time
  8. Eat it.
